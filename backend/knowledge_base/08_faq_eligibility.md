# Frequently Asked Questions — Eligibility

## Who Can Get Auto Insurance?

**Q: What is the minimum age for auto insurance?**
A: You must be at least 16 years old and hold a valid driver's license to purchase auto insurance from ShieldBase. Drivers under 18 may need a parent or guardian as a co-signer on the policy.

**Q: Do you cover all types of vehicles?**
A: We cover standard personal vehicles including cars, trucks, SUVs, vans, and motorcycles. We do not currently cover commercial vehicles, RVs, boats, or ATVs. The vehicle must have a model year between 1900 and the current year.

**Q: Can I get coverage with a bad driving record?**
A: Yes. ShieldBase provides coverage for drivers with all types of driving histories, including those with DUI convictions or at-fault accidents. However, your premium will be higher to reflect the increased risk. We classify driving history as Clean, Minor (1-2 minor violations like speeding tickets), or Major (at-fault accidents or DUI).

**Q: Do you cover rideshare drivers (Uber/Lyft)?**
A: Our standard auto policies do not cover commercial rideshare activity. We recommend a commercial auto policy for rideshare drivers, which we plan to offer in the future.

## Who Can Get Home Insurance?

**Q: Do you insure renters?**
A: Yes. Our apartment coverage option is designed for renters and covers personal belongings and liability, though not the building structure itself (that's the landlord's responsibility).

**Q: What property types do you cover?**
A: We cover houses, condos, and apartments. This includes single-family homes, townhouses, duplexes, and condominium units.

**Q: Is there a minimum or maximum property value?**
A: The minimum property value for a quote is $10,000. There is no maximum, but properties valued over $5,000,000 require a custom underwriting review.

**Q: Do you cover vacation homes or second properties?**
A: Yes, second homes and vacation properties can be insured under a separate policy. Each property requires its own policy.

## Who Can Get Life Insurance?

**Q: What is the age range for life insurance?**
A: Applicants must be between 18 and 75 years old at the time of application.

**Q: Is a medical exam required?**
A: No medical exam is required for coverage amounts under $250,000. For higher amounts, we may request a basic health screening.

**Q: What coverage amounts are available?**
A: Coverage ranges from a minimum of $10,000 to a maximum of $2,000,000. You can choose any amount within this range.

**Q: Can I have multiple life insurance policies?**
A: Yes, you can hold multiple policies with ShieldBase or combine our coverage with policies from other providers. However, total coverage across all providers typically should not exceed 15-20 times your annual income.

**Q: What term lengths do you offer?**
A: We offer 10-year, 20-year, and 30-year terms. The term you choose should align with your financial obligations and family needs.
