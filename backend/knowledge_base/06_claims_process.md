# Claims Process at ShieldBase

## Overview

Filing a claim with ShieldBase is designed to be simple, fast, and stress-free. Our average claims processing time is 48 hours from submission to resolution. We handle thousands of claims every month and pride ourselves on a 97% customer satisfaction rate for claims handling.

## How to File a Claim

### Step 1: Report the Incident
Contact us within 72 hours of the incident through any of these channels:
- **Online**: Log into your ShieldBase account and click "File a Claim"
- **Phone**: Call our 24/7 claims hotline at 1-800-SHIELD-1 (1-800-744-3531)
- **App**: Use the ShieldBase mobile app to file with photos and GPS location
- **Chat**: Start a claim through our AI chatbot on the website

### Step 2: Provide Documentation
Depending on your claim type, you may need to provide:
- **Auto claims**: Police report (if applicable), photos of damage, other driver's information, repair estimates
- **Home claims**: Photos of damage, receipts for damaged items, contractor estimates, police report for theft
- **Life claims**: Death certificate, policy number, beneficiary identification documents

### Step 3: Claim Review
A ShieldBase claims adjuster will review your submission within 24 hours. They may:
- Request additional documentation or photos
- Schedule an in-person inspection (for large claims)
- Contact third parties (other drivers, police, contractors)

### Step 4: Resolution
Once review is complete, we'll send you a settlement offer. For approved claims:
- **Auto**: Payment sent to your repair shop or directly to you
- **Home**: Payment issued via check or direct deposit
- **Life**: Death benefit paid to designated beneficiaries

## Important Claims Information

- **Deductible**: You are responsible for your deductible amount before coverage kicks in. Basic plans have higher deductibles; Comprehensive plans have lower ones.
- **No-fault states**: In no-fault states, your own policy covers your medical expenses regardless of who caused the accident.
- **Claim limits**: Payouts are capped at your policy's coverage limits. Ensure your coverage is adequate for your needs.
- **Fraud warning**: Filing a fraudulent claim is a felony. ShieldBase uses advanced fraud detection and cooperates fully with law enforcement.

## After Filing

You can track your claim status in real time through your online account or mobile app. Our claims team will proactively update you at each stage of the process. If you have questions about an active claim, contact your assigned adjuster directly.
