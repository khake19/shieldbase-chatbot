# ShieldBase Insurance — Company Overview

ShieldBase Insurance is a modern insurance provider founded in 2018, headquartered in Austin, Texas. We specialize in making insurance accessible, transparent, and affordable for individuals and families across the United States.

## Our Mission

At ShieldBase, we believe everyone deserves straightforward insurance coverage without the complexity and hidden fees that plague the traditional insurance industry. Our mission is to simplify insurance through technology while maintaining the personal touch our customers deserve.

## What We Offer

ShieldBase provides three core insurance products:

- **Auto Insurance**: Comprehensive vehicle coverage including liability, collision, and full comprehensive plans. We cover cars, trucks, SUVs, and motorcycles for drivers aged 16 and older.
- **Home Insurance**: Property protection for homeowners, condo owners, and apartment renters. Coverage includes structural damage, personal property, liability, and natural disaster add-ons.
- **Life Insurance**: Term life insurance plans with coverage amounts ranging from $10,000 to $2,000,000. Available in 10-year, 20-year, and 30-year terms.
