# Coverage Levels Explained

## Overview

ShieldBase offers three coverage levels across all insurance products: **Basic**, **Standard**, and **Comprehensive**. Each level provides increasing protection and benefits. Understanding the differences helps you choose the right plan for your needs and budget.

## Basic Coverage

Basic coverage provides essential protection at the lowest cost. It meets minimum requirements and covers the most common risks.

**Best for:**
- Budget-conscious customers who want minimum required coverage
- Older vehicles where full coverage isn't cost-effective
- Low-value properties or rentals
- Young, healthy individuals who want minimal life insurance

**What to expect:**
- Lower monthly premiums
- Higher deductibles when you file a claim
- Coverage limited to major perils and incidents
- Fewer additional benefits and add-ons

## Standard Coverage

Standard coverage is our most popular tier, chosen by approximately 60% of ShieldBase customers. It provides a strong balance between comprehensive protection and affordable pricing.

**Best for:**
- Most families and individuals
- Newer vehicles that need collision and comprehensive protection
- Homeowners who want solid property protection
- Individuals seeking moderate life insurance coverage

**What to expect:**
- Moderate monthly premiums
- Reasonable deductibles ($500 typical)
- Broad coverage including most common perils
- Additional benefits like roadside assistance (auto) and identity theft protection (home)

## Comprehensive Coverage

Comprehensive coverage is our premium tier, providing the widest protection with the lowest deductibles and highest limits.

**Best for:**
- Customers who want maximum peace of mind
- New or high-value vehicles
- High-value properties or homes with expensive contents
- Individuals with dependents who need maximum life insurance protection

**What to expect:**
- Higher monthly premiums (but best value per dollar of coverage)
- Low deductibles ($250 or less)
- Coverage for rare and unusual events
- Full replacement cost for property claims
- Premium benefits and add-ons included at no extra charge

## Comparing Coverage Levels

| Feature | Basic | Standard | Comprehensive |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly cost | Lowest | Moderate | Highest |
| Deductible | High | Medium | Low |
| Protection breadth | Essential | Broad | Maximum |
| Add-on benefits | Few | Several | All included |
| Customer popularity | 15% | 60% | 25% |

## Upgrading Your Coverage

You can upgrade your coverage level at any time during your policy period. Upgrades take effect immediately, and your premium is prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at your next renewal date.

Contact our support team or use the chatbot to explore how changing your coverage level affects your premium.
