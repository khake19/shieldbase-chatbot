# Auto Insurance at ShieldBase

## Overview

ShieldBase Auto Insurance protects you and your vehicle against accidents, theft, and liability. We offer coverage for personal vehicles including cars, trucks, SUVs, and motorcycles. Our policies are available to licensed drivers aged 16 and older.

## Coverage Levels

### Basic Coverage
Our Basic plan includes state-minimum liability coverage and uninsured motorist protection. This is ideal for older vehicles or budget-conscious drivers. Basic coverage includes:
- Bodily injury liability: $25,000 per person / $50,000 per accident
- Property damage liability: $25,000
- Uninsured motorist coverage

### Standard Coverage
The Standard plan adds collision and comprehensive protection on top of Basic coverage. Most of our customers choose this plan. Standard coverage includes everything in Basic, plus:
- Collision coverage with $500 deductible
- Comprehensive coverage (theft, vandalism, weather damage)
- Medical payments coverage: $5,000
- Roadside assistance

### Comprehensive Coverage
Our top-tier Comprehensive plan provides maximum protection with lower deductibles and higher limits. Comprehensive coverage includes everything in Standard, plus:
- Collision with $250 deductible
- Rental car reimbursement up to $50/day
- Gap coverage (pays difference between car value and loan balance)
- Enhanced liability limits: $100,000/$300,000
- New car replacement for vehicles under 2 years old

## Required Information for a Quote

To generate your auto insurance quote, we need the following details:
- **Your age**: Must be 16 or older
- **Vehicle year**: The model year of your vehicle (1900 to current year)
- **Driving history**: Clean (no incidents), Minor (1-2 minor violations), or Major (at-fault accidents or DUI)
- **Coverage level**: Basic, Standard, or Comprehensive

## Pricing Factors

Your auto insurance premium is calculated based on your age, driving history, and selected coverage level. Younger drivers (under 25) and older drivers (over 65) may see higher rates due to statistical risk factors. A clean driving record earns you the best rates, while major incidents significantly increase premiums.
