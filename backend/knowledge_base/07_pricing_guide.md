# Pricing Guide

## Overview

ShieldBase uses a transparent pricing model based on a set of clearly defined risk factors. We don't use credit scores or hidden algorithms. Your premium is calculated from your personal details and the coverage level you choose.

## Auto Insurance Pricing

Auto insurance premiums are based on four factors:

1. **Base rate**: $500/month before adjustments
2. **Age factor**: Drivers under 25 pay 50% more due to higher accident rates. Drivers 25-65 pay the standard rate. Drivers over 65 pay 30% more.
3. **Driving history factor**: Clean record gets a 10% discount. Minor violations add 20%. Major incidents (at-fault accidents, DUI) add 50%.
4. **Coverage level factor**: Basic saves 20%. Standard is the base rate. Comprehensive adds 50%.

**Example**: A 30-year-old with a clean record choosing Standard coverage would pay approximately $500 × 1.0 × 0.9 × 1.0 / 12 = **$37.50/month**.

**Example**: A 22-year-old with minor violations choosing Comprehensive coverage would pay approximately $500 × 1.5 × 1.2 × 1.5 / 12 = **$112.50/month**.

## Home Insurance Pricing

Home insurance premiums are based on three factors:

1. **Base rate**: 0.3% of your property value annually
2. **Property type factor**: Apartments pay 20% less. Houses pay the standard rate. Condos pay 10% less.
3. **Coverage level factor**: Basic saves 30%. Standard is the base rate. Comprehensive adds 40%.

**Example**: A house valued at $300,000 with Standard coverage would pay approximately $300,000 × 0.003 × 1.0 × 1.0 / 12 = **$75.00/month**.

**Example**: A condo valued at $200,000 with Comprehensive coverage would pay approximately $200,000 × 0.003 × 0.9 × 1.4 / 12 = **$63.00/month**.

## Life Insurance Pricing

Life insurance premiums are based on four factors:

1. **Base rate**: 0.2% of coverage amount annually
2. **Age factor**: Under 30 pays 20% less. Ages 30-50 pay the standard rate. Ages 50-65 pay 50% more. Over 65 pays 150% more.
3. **Health factor**: Excellent health gets a 20% discount. Good health is the standard rate. Fair health adds 30%. Poor health adds 80%.
4. **Term factor**: 10-year term saves 20%. 20-year term is the standard rate. 30-year term adds 30%.

**Example**: A healthy 35-year-old wanting $500,000 coverage for 20 years would pay approximately $500,000 × 0.002 × 1.0 × 1.0 × 1.0 / 12 = **$83.33/month**.

## Payment Options

- Monthly billing (no additional fee)
- Quarterly billing (1% discount)
- Annual billing (5% discount)
- Automatic payment via bank account or credit card

## Price Lock Guarantee

Your quoted premium is locked for the first 12 months of your policy. After that, rates may adjust at renewal based on claims history and market conditions, but ShieldBase guarantees rate increases will never exceed 10% per year.
