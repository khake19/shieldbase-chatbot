# Bundling & Discounts at ShieldBase

## Bundle Your Policies and Save

One of the easiest ways to save money on insurance is to bundle multiple policies together. ShieldBase offers significant discounts when you combine two or more insurance products.

## Bundle Discount Tiers

### Two-Policy Bundle (10% off)
Combine any two ShieldBase insurance products and receive a 10% discount on both policies. Popular combinations include:
- **Auto + Home**: The most common bundle. Protect your car and your home together.
- **Auto + Life**: Cover your vehicle and secure your family's financial future.
- **Home + Life**: Protect your property and your loved ones simultaneously.

### Three-Policy Bundle (15% off)
Insure your vehicle, home, and life all with ShieldBase and receive our maximum 15% bundle discount on all three policies. This is our best value offering and saves the average family over $400 per year compared to purchasing each policy separately.

## Other Available Discounts

### Auto Insurance Discounts
- **Safe Driver Discount (10%)**: Maintain a clean driving record for 3 or more consecutive years.
- **Multi-Vehicle Discount (5%)**: Insure more than one vehicle on the same policy.
- **Defensive Driving Course (5%)**: Complete an approved defensive driving course. Certificate must be submitted.
- **Low Mileage Discount (3%)**: Drive less than 7,500 miles per year.
- **Good Student Discount (8%)**: Full-time students under 25 with a GPA of 3.0 or higher.

### Home Insurance Discounts
- **Security System Discount (5%)**: Homes with monitored security systems qualify.
- **New Home Discount (7%)**: Homes built within the last 10 years.
- **Claims-Free Discount (10%)**: No claims filed in the past 5 years.
- **Protective Device Discount (3%)**: Smoke detectors, fire extinguishers, and deadbolt locks.

### Life Insurance Discounts
- **Non-Smoker Discount**: Already factored into the "Excellent" and "Good" health status rates.
- **Annual Payment Discount (5%)**: Pay your annual premium in one lump sum instead of monthly.
- **Loyalty Discount (3%)**: Available after 3 consecutive years as a ShieldBase policyholder.

## How Discounts Are Applied

Discounts are applied as a percentage reduction to your base premium after risk factors are calculated. Multiple discounts can be stacked, but total discounts are capped at 35% of the base premium.

## How to Get Your Bundle Set Up

1. Get individual quotes for each product you're interested in
2. Let your agent or our chatbot know you'd like to bundle
3. We'll automatically apply the bundle discount to all qualifying policies
4. See your combined savings instantly

Bundle discounts are applied at the policy level and reflected in your monthly billing. You can add products to your bundle at any time — the discount kicks in as soon as the new policy is active.

## Important Notes
- Bundle discounts require all policies to remain active. Cancelling one policy removes the bundle discount from remaining policies.
- Discounts apply to the premium after risk-based adjustments, not to the base rate.
- Some discounts require annual verification (e.g., good student, low mileage).
