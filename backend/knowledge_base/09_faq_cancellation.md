# Frequently Asked Questions — Cancellation & Changes

## Cancellation Policy

**Q: Can I cancel my policy at any time?**
A: Yes. ShieldBase policies can be cancelled at any time with no cancellation fees. Simply contact us by phone, email, or through your online account. Cancellation takes effect at midnight on the date you specify.

**Q: Will I get a refund if I cancel mid-term?**
A: Yes. If you've prepaid for your policy (quarterly or annually), you'll receive a prorated refund for the unused portion of your coverage period. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.

**Q: What happens to my coverage when I cancel?**
A: Coverage ends at midnight on your chosen cancellation date. Make sure you have replacement coverage in place before cancelling, especially for auto insurance (required by law in most states) and home insurance (often required by your mortgage lender).

**Q: Can ShieldBase cancel my policy?**
A: ShieldBase may cancel your policy for non-payment of premiums (after a 30-day grace period), fraud, or material misrepresentation on your application. We will always provide written notice at least 30 days before cancellation.

## Policy Changes

**Q: Can I change my coverage level?**
A: Yes. You can upgrade your coverage level at any time, and the change takes effect immediately. Your premium will be prorated. Downgrades take effect at your next renewal date.

**Q: Can I add or remove vehicles from my auto policy?**
A: Yes. Contact us to add or remove vehicles at any time. Adding a vehicle adjusts your premium immediately. Removing a vehicle takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

**Q: Can I change my beneficiary on my life insurance?**
A: Yes. You can update your beneficiary designation at any time by logging into your account or contacting our support team. Changes are effective immediately upon processing.

**Q: What if my circumstances change (new home, marriage, etc.)?**
A: Life changes can affect your insurance needs and premiums. We recommend reviewing your policies after major life events such as:
- Moving to a new home
- Getting married or divorced
- Having children
- Purchasing a new vehicle
- Significant change in income
- Retirement

Contact us to review and adjust your coverage accordingly.

## Renewal

**Q: Does my policy auto-renew?**
A: Yes. All ShieldBase policies auto-renew at the end of each term unless you request cancellation. We'll send you a renewal notice 30 days before your renewal date with any premium changes.

**Q: Can my premium change at renewal?**
A: Yes, premiums may adjust at renewal based on claims history, market conditions, and risk factors. However, ShieldBase guarantees that rate increases will never exceed 10% per year.

**Q: What if I miss a payment?**
A: You have a 30-day grace period after your payment due date. During the grace period, your coverage remains active. If payment is not received within 30 days, your policy may be cancelled. Contact us immediately if you're having difficulty making payments — we can often work out a payment arrangement.
