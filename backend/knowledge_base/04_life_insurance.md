# Life Insurance at ShieldBase

## Overview

ShieldBase Life Insurance provides financial protection for your loved ones in the event of your passing. We offer term life insurance policies that pay a death benefit to your designated beneficiaries. Our policies are available to individuals aged 18 to 75, with coverage amounts ranging from $10,000 to $2,000,000.

## How Term Life Insurance Works

Term life insurance provides coverage for a specific period (the "term"). If you pass away during the term, your beneficiaries receive the full death benefit, tax-free. If the term expires and you're still living, the policy simply ends with no payout. Term life is the most affordable type of life insurance.

## Available Terms

- **10-Year Term**: Best for short-term needs like covering a mortgage or while children are young. This is our most affordable option.
- **20-Year Term**: Our most popular choice. Provides a balance of long-term coverage and reasonable premiums. Ideal for families with growing children.
- **30-Year Term**: Maximum protection period. Best for younger applicants who want to lock in low rates for decades. Covers you through major life milestones.

## Required Information for a Quote

To generate your life insurance quote, we need:
- **Your age**: Must be between 18 and 75
- **Coverage amount**: The death benefit amount, minimum $10,000 up to $2,000,000
- **Health status**: Excellent, Good, Fair, or Poor (based on general health assessment)
- **Term length**: 10, 20, or 30 years

## Health Status Guidelines

- **Excellent**: No chronic conditions, non-smoker, regular exercise, healthy BMI, no family history of major illness
- **Good**: Generally healthy, may have minor controlled conditions (e.g., mild allergies), non-smoker or former smoker (quit 5+ years ago)
- **Fair**: One or more managed chronic conditions (e.g., controlled hypertension, type 2 diabetes), or current smoker, or moderately overweight
- **Poor**: Multiple chronic conditions, uncontrolled health issues, morbid obesity, or high-risk lifestyle factors

## Pricing Factors

Life insurance premiums are calculated based on your age, health status, coverage amount, and term length. Younger, healthier individuals receive significantly lower rates. Choosing a longer term or higher coverage amount increases premiums proportionally.

## Key Benefits
- Guaranteed level premiums — your rate never increases during the term
- No medical exam required for coverage under $250,000
- Beneficiary receives 100% tax-free death benefit
- Coverage is portable — stays with you if you change jobs
- Optional accelerated death benefit rider for terminal illness
