# Home Insurance at ShieldBase

## Overview

ShieldBase Home Insurance provides comprehensive property protection for homeowners, condo owners, and apartment residents. Our policies cover structural damage, personal belongings, liability, and additional living expenses if your home becomes uninhabitable.

## Coverage Levels

### Basic Coverage
Our Basic home insurance plan covers essential perils including fire, lightning, windstorm, hail, and theft. Basic coverage includes:
- Dwelling coverage up to assessed property value
- Personal property coverage at 50% of dwelling coverage
- Liability protection: $100,000
- Additional living expenses: up to 20% of dwelling coverage

### Standard Coverage
The Standard plan expands protection to include more perils and higher limits. Standard coverage includes everything in Basic, plus:
- Water damage from burst pipes and appliance overflow
- Falling objects and weight of ice/snow
- Personal property coverage at 70% of dwelling coverage
- Liability protection: $300,000
- Medical payments to others: $5,000
- Identity theft recovery: $25,000

### Comprehensive Coverage
Our Comprehensive plan offers the broadest protection available. Comprehensive coverage includes everything in Standard, plus:
- Personal property at full replacement cost (not depreciated value)
- Scheduled personal property coverage for high-value items (jewelry, art, electronics)
- Liability protection: $500,000
- Ordinance or law coverage (rebuilding to current code)
- Sewer and drain backup coverage
- Equipment breakdown coverage

## Required Information for a Quote

To generate your home insurance quote, we need:
- **Property value**: The current market value of your property (minimum $10,000)
- **Property type**: House, Condo, or Apartment
- **Coverage level**: Basic, Standard, or Comprehensive

## Pricing Factors

Home insurance premiums are primarily based on your property's value and type. Houses generally cost more to insure than condos or apartments due to full structural responsibility. The coverage level you choose determines the breadth of protection and directly impacts your premium.

## Additional Options
- Flood insurance add-on (required in FEMA flood zones)
- Earthquake coverage add-on
- Home business coverage
- Valuable items scheduling (for items over $2,500)
